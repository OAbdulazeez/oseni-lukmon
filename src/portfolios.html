Lagos State Revenue Board

We have worked for Lagos State Internal Revenue Board as Consultants for Accelerated Revenue Generation Programme from 2010 to date . The following Services were rendered:

(a) Ensuring that all the Provisions of Act 104 of 1993 particularly sections 67, 68, 69, 70, 71, 72, 80 and 81 are adhered to by all registered employers.

(b) Educating Organization on how to comply with the relevant sections of Personal Income tax Act 104 of 1993 including record keeping and remittance.

(c) Carrying out the inspection of the relevant books and records of the Organization in accordance with section 46 (4) of Act 1 of 1993; and

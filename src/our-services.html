The company conducts statutory audits mandated by the Companies and Allied Matters Decree 1990, as amended. These audits entail examining and expressing opinions, based on sampled evidence, regarding the accounts, significant estimates, and judgments made by directors in preparing financial statements.

(d) Prospective reports for new issue of share We also go further than expressing opinion by making our audit to serve as management input for strategic and short plans for overall goal accomplishment.
